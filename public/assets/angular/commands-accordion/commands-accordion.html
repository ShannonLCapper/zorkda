Look / L
Gives you information about the room you are in
Next / N / Wait
Moves time forward one frame
Health / Hearts / H
Shows you your health
Heart pieces
Shows how many heart pieces you have
Rupees / Wallet / Money / R
Shows you how many rupees you have
Inventory / I
Shows you your inventory
Equipment / E
Shows you your equipment
Carrying
Tells you what you're carrying in front of you, if anything
Navi / Talk to Navi
Listens to Navi if she calls to you, otherwise gives objective
Enemy Info
Gives you information about any enemies in the room
Stones / Spiritual Stones
Shows you what spiritual stones you have
Medallions
Shows you what medallions you have
Tokens / Gold Skulltula Tokens
Shows you how many gold skulltula tokens you have
Brief
Long room descriptions will not show for rooms you have already visited
Verbose
Restores long room descriptions

Go <direction>
Moves you to a new room, if able
  • Valid directions: north, south, east, west, up, down
  • Abbreviations are acceptable (ie. "go n")
Talk to / Speak to <person>
Allows you to speak to a character in the room
Pick up / Get <item>
Picks up an item in the room
  • If the item can be added to your inventory, it will do so
  • Otherwise, you will carry the item in front of you
Put down / Drop <item> (on <item>)
Puts down an item you are holding
  • If the optional "on <item>" is used, will drop the item on something in particular
Throw <item> (at <enemy / item>)
Roughly throws an item that you are holding in front of you
  • If the item breaks, you will be given its contents, if it has any
Open <chest>
Opens a chest, if able
  • Do not use this action on doors. They will open automatically if you walk in their direction
Step on / Press <switch>
Presses a floor switch temporarily
Break <item> with <sword / inventory item>
Breaks item and gives you its contents, if it has any
Buy <item>
Buys an item in a shop, if you have sufficient rupees
Light <item> with <item>
Lights an object on fire using another already lit object
  • You can use this to light flammable items from your inventory on fire
  • Or you can light objects in the room on fire using something on fire in your inventory
Extinguish stick / deku stick
Puts out your lit deku stick, if you have one
Push / Slide <item>
Pushes around an object in the room
Dive for <item>
Reaches for an item submerged underwater

Hit <enemy / object> with <item>
Hits an enemy or object in the room with an item you possess
  • If the enemy dies, you will automatically be given any items it drops
Shoot <enemy / object> with <item>
Shoots an enemy or object in the room with a weapon you possess
Shoot <item> at <enemy / object>
Shoots an enemy or object in the room with a weapon you possess
Cut <item>
Cuts target with your equipped sword
Block
Blocks incoming attacks with your shield
Dodge
Dodges incoming attacks
